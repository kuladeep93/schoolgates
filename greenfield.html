Admission Procedure

  1. Please collect the admission form from the school office or download from the School website after registering your ward
  2. Submit the completed admission form along with the required details, documents and fee at the school office
  3. Admission forms must be completed by the parents and same would be accepted only from the parents
  4. Submit an attested copy of the birth certificate & progress report of the grade studied
  5. Kindly fill the transportation form if the student likes to avail school transporting facility
  6. Uniform, books, time table and other schedules can be obtained from the school admin office and collect the same as per the schedule
  7. Make sure the presence of your ward on the scheduled day of commencemen
