About us

Green Field School International is a novel venture of Sri Dhanalakshmi Educational Society, Kakinada Andhra Pradesh to pedal the process of educational transformation with strong International practices attuned by Indian values. Green Field School is located at Vakada, 13 km away from Kakinada Town, the District Head quarters of East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh in a lush green campus spread over 6 acres of land just adjacent to the main road. Green Field School International keeps its focus honed in pursuance of the skills required for application, analysis, synthesis, evaluation and creation of knowledge.The school has the conviction and commitment to equip the future generation with :

  1. a strong secular ethos to understand the diversity of any society
  2. an integrated approach and a vision in life
  3. leadership qualities
  4. environmental sensitivity

To educate and inspire students to be responsible, productive and ethical world citizens with the skills and passion to think creatively, reason critically, communicate effectively and learn continuously.

  1. Committed to realizing the innate potential of each child to cope with future challenges.
  2. Innovative, Creative and Child-centric teaching-learning process
  3. Positive and innovative learning environment that encourages every learner to be a lifelong learner
  4. Provision for technological access to achieve 21st century skills

Kindergarten

Kindergarten students need special attention and affection not only from their parents but also from their teachers. School employs flexible and suitable methods for kindergartens for their first stage of learning as it makes the foundation for the rest of education.
Green Field School International follows,

  1. Montessori method in classrooms
  2. Maximum care for the children
  3. Exposure to morals and values
  4. Learn the skills for effective communication
  5. Well-equipped & comfortable classrooms for easy learning

Secondary School

Green Field School International instills practical learning in students with the best faculty for every subject, with computer lab, physics lab, chemistry lab and biology lab facilities. Every class is with minimum number of students so that teachers can concentrate on every students learning curve and help them to cope up with the competition of the class. As they get ready to step out of the school world, it is important to ready them for the competitive world with the right kind of knowledge and attitude towards learning
